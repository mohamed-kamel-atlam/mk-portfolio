## Overview

Indoor Plante is a modern, responsive e-commerce storefront dedicated to bringing nature into homes and offices, offering high-quality indoor plants with fast delivery, attentive customer service, and a 30-day trial guarantee. The build showcases a fully designed plant shop with smooth animations, full responsiveness across devices, SEO-optimized content, a product showcase, a customer review carousel, and newsletter subscription integration.

## Features

- Responsive design tuned for mobile, tablet, and desktop
- Optimized assets and smooth animations for fast perceived performance
- SEO optimization with complete meta tags and structured data
- Mobile-first build approach
- Scroll-reveal animations for engaging, progressive content disclosure
- E-commerce essentials: product showcase, customer reviews, and newsletter signup
- Intuitive navigation with clear calls-to-action

## Page Structure & Layout

The storefront is organized into distinct, purpose-built sections:

- **Header & navigation** - fixed navbar with a hamburger menu for mobile, and active-link highlighting tied to scroll position
- **Home/hero** - headline, subheading, call-to-action buttons, and a featured plant visual
- **Services** - four cards highlighting key benefits in a responsive grid
- **About** - company story and mission with supporting feature highlights
- **Popular plants** - a responsive product grid (from a single column up to four columns) with pricing, hover animations, and star ratings
- **Reviews** - customer testimonials in a Swiper-powered carousel with pagination, laid out as a responsive 3-column grid on larger screens
- **Newsletter** - email subscription form with supporting call-to-action copy
- **Footer** - social links, quick navigation, and company information

## Design & UX

Typography pairs a **Lobster** display face for headings with **Jost** for body copy, scaling responsively from `3xl` to `5xl` at larger breakpoints. The color system centers on a deep green primary, a brighter green secondary, a warm yellow accent, a charcoal dark background, and an off-white text color for contrast.

Layout is constrained to a 1200px max-width container, with responsive padding and Tailwind-driven gap spacing. Grids reflow across breakpoints - from a single-column mobile layout, through 2-column tablet grids, up to a 4-column layout on laptop-and-larger viewports - so the product and review grids stay legible at every size.

## Highlights

- Interaction details handled in vanilla JavaScript: toggleable hamburger menu, smooth in-page scrolling, dynamic active-link highlighting, a scroll-up button that appears past a scroll threshold, and a header shadow that appears on scroll
- Swiper.js powers the product and review carousels with pagination controls
- CSS animation layer includes fade-in scroll reveals, hover scale effects, translate transitions, and rotate/scale-bounce accents
- SEO groundwork includes meta descriptions, keyword optimization, Open Graph and Twitter Card tags, canonical URLs, semantic HTML5 structure, and alt text on imagery

## Tech Stack

Built with semantic HTML5 and Tailwind CSS for utility-first styling, with vanilla JavaScript (ES6+) driving interactivity. Swiper.js handles the product/review carousels, ScrollReveal.js drives scroll animations, Remix Icons supplies the icon set, and Google Fonts provides the Jost and Lobster typefaces.
