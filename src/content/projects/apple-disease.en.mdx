## Overview

A deep learning model that detects plant diseases, with a focus on apple diseases, from leaf images. The goal is to make disease diagnosis fast and accessible so that early, timely intervention becomes possible.

## Problem

Undetected plant diseases lead to reduced crop yields and delayed treatment. Early detection helps:

- **Farmers** take timely action, reducing crop losses and supporting more sustainable agriculture through interactive disease diagnosis.
- **Agricultural experts and researchers** identify diseases in experimental settings and analyze disease prevalence and patterns across plant species.
- **Students and educators** use the system as an educational resource for understanding plant diseases and applied deep learning techniques.

## Dataset

The dataset is sourced from the New Plant Diseases Dataset, which contains roughly 87.9k images across many plant species. This project uses only the apple subset, comprising 7,780 images.

## Approach / Methodology

**Data preparation**: images are normalized with `transforms.ToTensor()`, and a `DataLoader` is used for efficient batch processing during training.

**Model and training**: a convolutional neural network (CNN) performs the image recognition and classification. Training uses:

- The Adam optimizer, for per-parameter adaptive learning rates.
- A learning rate schedule controlling the step size at each iteration toward the loss minimum.
- `CrossEntropyLoss()` as the classification loss function.

**Evaluation**: training progress is tracked via accuracy and loss curves, precision/recall/F1 per epoch, and a confusion matrix, in addition to testing the trained model against held-out images to verify its predictions.

## Results

The model achieved 97% accuracy on the validation dataset and 92.5% accuracy on the test dataset.

## Future Work

- Integrating additional plant-related datasets to improve the model's accuracy and generalization.
- Deploying on cloud platforms for scalability and increased availability.
- Incorporating real-time image augmentation and preprocessing to handle varying image qualities and formats.
- Building a mobile app version for on-the-go plant disease diagnosis.
